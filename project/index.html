About Print3d

Print3d is a small business dedicated to selling 3D-printed sculptures of custom LDS temples, LDS decorative sculptures, and religious sculptures to bring the spirit into the home. We take special orders and dedications and focus on improving the presentation of our models.

Precision and Detail in Every Model

Our printing process strives to make the models as detailed as possible; they are polished and detailed to closely resemble the original sculptures and structures on which they are based.

Built with Passion and Purpose

Our sculptures are not only meant to be more than just decorative for the home; we aim for them to be a tribute and reminder of faith and to bring the spirit into the home.
